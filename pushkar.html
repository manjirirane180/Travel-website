1-Day Pushkar Itinerary
Discover the spiritual and cultural essence of Pushkar in a single day.
Morning: Start with a visit to the sacred Brahma Temple, one of the few temples dedicated to Lord Brahma. Stroll along the ghats of Pushkar Lake and witness the morning rituals.
Afternoon: Explore the bustling Pushkar Bazaar, known for handicrafts, jewelry, and traditional Rajasthani attire. Enjoy a local Rajasthani thali for lunch.
Evening: Hike to Savitri Temple for a breathtaking sunset view over the desert landscape. End your day by experiencing the peaceful Aarti at Pushkar Lake.

2-Day Pushkar Itinerary
Enjoy the best of Pushkar’s spirituality, culture, and desert adventure with this 2-day plan.
Day 1: Begin your journey at the Brahma Temple and take a peaceful walk around Pushkar Lake. Explore the vibrant Pushkar Bazaar for souvenirs. In the evening, visit Savitri Temple for panoramic sunset views.
Day 2: Start your day with an early morning camel safari in the Thar Desert. Visit the serene Varaha Temple and Gurudwara Sahib. Spend your afternoon relaxing at a rooftop café overlooking the ghats, soaking in Pushkar’s tranquil atmosphere.
