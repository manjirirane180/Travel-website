3-Day Jaisalmer Itinerary
Immerse yourself in the rich heritage and desert magic of Jaisalmer with this extended 3-day itinerary.
Day 1: Begin your journey at Jaisalmer Fort and explore the vibrant old town. Visit Patwon Ki Haveli and marvel at its intricate architecture. In the evening, take a boat ride at Gadisar Lake and enjoy a peaceful sunset.
Day 2: Spend the day at Kuldhara, the abandoned village with mysterious legends. Later, venture into the Thar Desert for a camel safari and desert camping experience, complete with cultural performances and a delicious Rajasthani dinner.
Day 3: Explore the grand Bada Bagh cenotaphs and the stunning Jain Temples inside Jaisalmer Fort. Relax at a rooftop caf√© and indulge in local delicacies before bidding farewell to this golden paradise.
