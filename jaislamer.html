2-Day Jaisalmer Itinerary
Explore the golden city of Jaisalmer with this perfect 2-day itinerary, covering its grand forts, havelis, and desert adventures.
Day 1: Start with a visit to the majestic Jaisalmer Fort, a living fort bustling with shops and temples. Explore the intricately designed Patwon Ki Haveli and Nathmal Ki Haveli. Enjoy lunch at a rooftop café with a view of the golden city. In the evening, head to Gadisar Lake for a serene sunset boat ride.
Day 2: Embark on a thrilling desert safari in the Sam Sand Dunes. Enjoy an adventurous camel ride, witness the mesmerizing sunset, and experience a traditional Rajasthani cultural night with folk dance and music under the stars.

3-Day Jaisalmer Itinerary
Immerse yourself in the rich heritage and desert magic of Jaisalmer with this extended 3-day itinerary.
Day 1: Begin your journey at Jaisalmer Fort and explore the vibrant old town. Visit Patwon Ki Haveli and marvel at its intricate architecture. In the evening, take a boat ride at Gadisar Lake and enjoy a peaceful sunset.
Day 2: Spend the day at Kuldhara, the abandoned village with mysterious legends. Later, venture into the Thar Desert for a camel safari and desert camping experience, complete with cultural performances and a delicious Rajasthani dinner.
Day 3: Explore the grand Bada Bagh cenotaphs and the stunning Jain Temples inside Jaisalmer Fort. Relax at a rooftop café and indulge in local delicacies before bidding farewell to this golden paradise.
