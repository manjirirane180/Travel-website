# Travel-website
Padharo Sa is a travel website that showcases the rich heritage, vibrant culture, and must-visit destinations of Rajasthan. This website provides information on top tourist spots, including: 📍 Jaipur – The Pink City 📍 Jaisalmer – The Golden City 📍 Jodhpur – The Blue City 📍 Udaipur – The City of Lakes 📍 Bikaner – The Desert Gem 📍 Pushkar
