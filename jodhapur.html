2-Day Jodhpur Itinerary

Explore the Blue City of Jodhpur, filled with royal palaces, historic forts, and vibrant markets.

Day 1: Begin your journey at the mighty Mehrangarh Fort, offering breathtaking views of Jodhpur. Explore the intricate architecture of Jaswant Thada and the grand Umaid Bhawan Palace. Enjoy a traditional Rajasthani lunch, followed by an evening stroll in the bustling Sardar Market near the iconic Clock Tower.

Day 2: Start with a peaceful sunrise at Kaylana Lake. Visit the historic Mandore Gardens and the serene Rao Jodha Desert Rock Park. Spend your afternoon shopping for handicrafts at the local bazaars before savoring an authentic Rajasthani dinner.
