2-Day Bikaner Itinerary
Explore the royal city of Bikaner in two days, discovering its grand forts, temples, and rich heritage.
Day 1: Start with Junagarh Fort, known for its impressive architecture. Visit the Karni Mata Temple, famous for its sacred rats. Explore the Rampuria Havelis, showcasing intricate craftsmanship. In the evening, enjoy camel rides and a desert sunset at the Sand Dunes near Bikaner.
Day 2: Visit the grand Laxmi Niwas Palace and explore Gajner Palace by the lakeside. Stroll through Bhandasar Jain Temple, known for its stunning frescoes. End your trip at Bikaner’s bustling markets, shopping for handicrafts and Bikaneri snacks.

3-Day Bikaner Itinerary
Experience Bikaner’s royal heritage, vibrant culture, and desert charm with this 3-day plan.
Day 1: Explore the magnificent Junagarh Fort and its grand palaces. Visit the sacred Karni Mata Temple, then admire the stunning Rampuria Havelis. In the evening, witness a desert sunset with camel safaris.
Day 2: Start at Laxmi Niwas Palace and Gajner Palace. Explore Bhandasar Jain Temple’s intricate frescoes. Visit the National Research Centre on Camel to learn about camel breeding.
Day 3: Discover Bikaner’s vibrant markets, famous for snacks and crafts. Visit Shiv Bari Temple, known for its red sandstone structure. End your journey with a relaxing evening at Gajner Lake, soaking in the serene views.
