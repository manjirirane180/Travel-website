2-Day Udaipur Itinerary
Explore Udaipur, the "City of Lakes," in two days with a mix of history, architecture, and scenic beauty.
Day 1: Start at the grand City Palace, overlooking Lake Pichola. Visit Jagdish Temple, famous for its intricate carvings. Take a boat ride on Lake Pichola to admire the Lake Palace and Jag Mandir. In the evening, watch a Rajasthani folk dance show at Bagore Ki Haveli.
Day 2: Walk around Fateh Sagar Lake and visit the serene Saheliyon Ki Bari gardens. Head to Monsoon Palace for stunning city views. Explore Shilpgram, a hub of rural arts and crafts. End your trip with a sunset at Ambrai Ghat, soaking in the city's illuminated beauty.
