2-Day Jaipur Itinerary

A 2-day stay in Jaipur offers a perfect mix of history, culture, and vibrant markets.

Day 1: Start with a visit to the majestic Amber Fort, followed by the iconic Hawa Mahal and the royal City Palace. Enjoy a traditional Rajasthani lunch and explore the Jantar Mantar observatory. In the evening, stroll through Johari Bazaar for souvenirs and experience a cultural show at Chokhi Dhani.

Day 2: Visit the serene Nahargarh Fort for a breathtaking sunrise, then explore the beautiful Albert Hall Museum. Spend your afternoon at the Birla Mandir and the scenic Jal Mahal. End your trip with authentic Rajasthani cuisine before bidding farewell to the Pink City.
